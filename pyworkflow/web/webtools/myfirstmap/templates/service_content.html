  • Upload and import your files by clicking on the import averages box. Finally press "Execute"!

  • Select a method to obtain your initial map . You can choose among xmipp - ransac, eman initial volume and/or xmipp - significant.



    Parameters can be easily customized. Two levels of usages are supported: Normal or Advanced, which should be chosen according to your experience with each of the methods



    Finally press "Execute"!

  • Once a method has finished, click on its box to show the summary of Inputs and Outputs.



    Click on the Analyze Results button to further analyze the results of each method.


  • The outputs of several methods can be mutually aligned in 3D and presented to the user for further selection using the align volumes box. Note that you should select one of the output 3D volumes to be used as a 3D reference. Finally press "Execute"!

    Aligned volumes can be downloaded using the corresponding button .
