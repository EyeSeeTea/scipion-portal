JAVI's method short description.

The overfitting method will assess the validity of a reconstruction from micrograph images of presumably identical particles based on aligned noise. This is both a sanity check for the microscopist at an early stage in a project, as well as for reconstructions already deposited in the EMDB. Practically, if the resolution of the reconstruction based on the images is not considerably different from the one based on the aligned gaussian noise (for less number of particles), the images may not produce a valid reconstruction (J. Bernard Heymann, 2015).
