  • Import volumes

    Import your volume file into the web portal by clicking on the graph on the left hand side of this panel, on the Import Volumes box.



    Once uploaded, press "Execute"!

  • Import aligned particles

    Import your particles files into the web portal by clicking on the graph on the left hand side of this panel, on the Import particles box. For this particular workflow the particles need to be aligned. Typically .stk and .star files (with alignment information) from Relion can be used. Pay special attention to the Import from dropdown list to choose the right format: relion (.stk + .star files), xmipp, freealign, emx.



    Once uploaded, press "Execute"!

  • Downsample your volumes (Optional)

    Downsample the volume to reduce the processing time of the later protocols.

  • Downsample your particles (Optional)

    Downsample the particles to reduce the processing time of the later protocols.

  • Validate non tilt

    Select the validate non-tilt box.

    Parameters can be easily customized. Two level of usages are supported (Normal and Advanced), which should be chosen according to your experience.


    Press "Execute"!


  • Validate overfitting

    Select the validate overfitting box.

    Parameters can be easily customized. Two level of usages are supported (Normal and Advanced), which should be chosen according to your experience.


    Finally press "Execute"!

  • Analyze teh results

    Method's results can be further analyzed by first clicking on (selecting) the desired Method box and then clicking on the Analyze Results button.
